About Foundation Scholars

Excellence in education, leadership, and community

Our Mission

The Foundation Scholars program at Western Michigan University is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, leadership development, and community engagement. We provide a supportive network for current students and alumni, creating lasting connections that extend far beyond graduation.

What is Foundation Scholars?

Foundation Scholars is a prestigious scholarship program that recognizes and supports outstanding students at Western Michigan University. Our scholars represent the best and brightest, demonstrating exceptional academic achievement, leadership potential, and commitment to community service.

Program Benefits

  • Financial Support: Comprehensive scholarship funding to support your education
  • Mentorship: Access to alumni mentors and professional guidance
  • Networking: Exclusive events connecting current scholars with successful alumni
  • Professional Development: Workshops, seminars, and career preparation resources
  • Community: A tight-knit community of like-minded scholars and lifelong friends
  • Alumni Network: Continued connection and support after graduation

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, leadership, and service.

Community

We build strong, supportive relationships that last a lifetime.

Leadership

We develop leaders who make positive impacts in their communities.

Service

We are committed to giving back and making a difference.

Join Our Legacy

Whether you're a current scholar or an alumnus, you're part of the Foundation Scholars family. Our network continues to grow, with scholars making their mark in various fields including business, education, healthcare, technology, and public service.

We invite you to stay connected, share your experiences, and support the next generation of Foundation Scholars. Together, we continue the tradition of excellence at Western Michigan University.
