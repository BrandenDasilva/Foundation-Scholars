Fall 2025 Networking Event - Save the Date!

We're excited to announce our Fall 2025 Networking Event scheduled for December 5th at the Western Michigan University Student Center. This year's event will feature keynote speaker Dr. Sarah Johnson (Class of 2015), now a leading researcher in sustainable energy.

  • Keynote address from distinguished alumni
  • Panel discussion on career pathways
  • Speed networking sessions
  • Dinner reception

Scholar Spotlight: Celebrating Recent Achievements

We're proud to highlight the incredible accomplishments of our Foundation Scholars this semester:

  • Academic Excellence: 15 scholars achieved 4.0 GPA this semester
  • Research Recognition: Three scholars presented at the National Undergraduate Research Conference
  • Community Service: Our scholars contributed over 500 volunteer hours to local organizations
  • Leadership Awards: Two scholars received WMU's Outstanding Student Leader Award

These achievements reflect the dedication and excellence that define our Foundation Scholars community. Congratulations to all our scholars!

Welcome

Welcome Class of 2029!

We are thrilled to welcome our newest cohort of Foundation Scholars! This year, we received a record number of applications and selected 25 outstanding students representing diverse backgrounds and academic interests.

Our new scholars come from across Michigan and bring expertise in fields ranging from engineering and computer science to arts and humanities. They join a legacy of excellence and community that spans over two decades.

Please join us in welcoming these exceptional students to the Foundation Scholars family!

July 10, 2025
Program

New Mentorship Program Launch

We're excited to announce the launch of our enhanced Foundation Scholars Mentorship Program! This program connects current scholars with alumni mentors for guidance, support, and professional development.

For Current Scholars: Gain insights from alumni who have walked in your shoes, receive career guidance, and build valuable professional connections.

For Alumni: Give back to the program, share your experiences, and help shape the next generation of Foundation Scholars.

Interested in participating? Contact us to learn more about becoming a mentor or mentee.
